This Privacy Policy describes how **BMI Calculator** (the “App”) handles information when you use it. The App is provided by the developer identified on the **Google Play** store listing (“we”, “us”). This policy is provided for transparency and to support store requirements; it is **not** legal advice.

**Last updated:** April 10, 2026

## Scope

This policy applies to the **BMI Calculator** mobile application distributed through Google Play (and, if offered, other builds from the same open-source project). If you use a **web** version of the app, the same general principles apply: inputs are used to compute BMI **on your device** in the published configuration described below.

## Summary

BMI Calculator is designed to work **primarily on your device**. In the current version of the app as published from this project, **height, weight, age, and gender** (or similar inputs you choose) are used **locally** to calculate Body Mass Index (BMI) and to show category labels and short informational text. **We do not operate a backend service that receives this health or body-related information from the App** in that configuration, and the published app **does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs** in its dependencies.

## Information the App processes

The App may process the following **on your device**:

- **Inputs you enter** — for example height, weight, age, and gender selection, used to compute BMI and display results and advice.
- **Technical data** needed to run the user interface (for example layout and rendering), as handled by the operating system and the Flutter framework.

We **do not** use these inputs to build a profile about you on **our** servers, because we **do not** receive them from the App in the ordinary operation described above.

## Accounts, cloud sync, and storage

The App **does not require an account** to use core features in the published configuration. **We do not provide cloud sync** of your BMI inputs or results to servers we control as part of this open-source build. Data may exist temporarily in **device memory** while you use the App. **Device backup** features (for example Android backup) may copy app data according to your **device and Google account settings**; that is governed by your platform provider’s policies, not by this policy alone.

## Network and internet access

Your device may have network capability for **operating system updates**, **play store updates**, or **platform features** unrelated to your BMI inputs. The App **does not** use a network connection to **upload your BMI-related inputs or results** to our servers in the published open-source configuration. If you later install a **modified build** or **add third-party packages** that change this behavior, you should follow the privacy policy that applies to **that** version.

## Third parties

- **Google Play** and **Google** may collect usage, device, and diagnostic information when you install or use apps from Play. That processing is described in **Google’s** privacy policies. We do not control Google’s data practices.
- **Apple** or other stores may apply similar rules if you use builds on other platforms.

## Children’s privacy

The App is **not directed at children** under the age required by your jurisdiction (for example **13** in the United States). Parents and guardians should supervise use of health-related tools by minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the App in a way that requires **account registration** in the version described here.

## Health and medical information

**BMI is a general screening measure.** It is **not** a medical diagnosis. The App is for **informational purposes only** and is **not** a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The App is **not** presented as a regulated medical device unless you separately qualify and label it as such in your jurisdiction.

## International users

If you use the App outside your country of residence, information may be processed in line with your **device**, **app store**, and **operating system** providers’ infrastructure. By using the App, you understand this policy applies in a reasonable way for a small utility application.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The **Last updated** date at the top will change when we do. **Continued use** of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy, unless applicable law requires otherwise.

## Contact

For privacy questions about this App, contact the developer using the **email address or support link shown on the Google Play store listing** for this app. You may also open an issue on the project’s public repository if you use that channel for feedback.
